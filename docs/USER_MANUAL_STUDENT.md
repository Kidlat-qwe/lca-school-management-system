## Introduction

This manual is specifically designed for **Student** users of the Physical School Management System. As a Student, you have access to view your classes, invoices, payments, and announcements. You can monitor your enrollment, attendance, and payment status.

### Key Characteristics of Student Role

- **Access Level**: Personal information only (your own data)
- **Primary Functions**: 
  - View enrolled classes
  - View invoices and payment status
  - Track payment history
  - Read announcements
  - View class schedules
  - Monitor attendance
- **Limitations**: 
  - Cannot create or modify anything
  - View-only access
  - Cannot access other students' information
  - Cannot modify classes or enrollments
  - Cannot create invoices or payments

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

3. Review your Dashboard

5. Review any pending invoices

**Phase Information**

- See all invoices assigned to you
- Filter by Status:
  - **Paid**: Fully paid invoices (green badge)
  - **Pending**: Awaiting payment (yellow badge)
  - **Unpaid**: Not paid yet (red badge)
  - **Overdue**: Past due date (red badge)
  - **Partially Paid**: Some payment received (yellow badge)

**Invoice Table Columns**

- Invoice Number (INV-XXXX)
- Description
- Amount
- Status (color-coded):
  - **Paid**: Green background
  - **Pending**: Yellow background
  - **Unpaid**: Red background
  - **Overdue**: Red background
  - **Partially Paid**: Yellow background
- Due Date
- Actions

**Viewing Invoice Details**

1. Click on invoice number or "View" button
2. See comprehensive invoice information:

**Invoice Information**
- Invoice number (INV-XXXX)
- Invoice description
- Issue date (when invoice was created)
- Due date (when payment is due)
- Status (Paid, Pending, Unpaid, Overdue, Partially Paid)

**Items Breakdown**
- List of items you're paying for
- Each item shows:
  - Description
  - Amount
  - Tax (if applicable)
  - Discount (if applicable)
  - Penalty (if applicable)
- Subtotal
- Tax total
- Discount total
- **Total Amount**: Final amount to pay

**Payment Information**
- **Total Amount**: Original invoice amount
- **Amount Paid**: Total payments received
- **Remaining Balance**: Amount still due
- **Payment History**: 
  - List of payments made
  - Payment dates
  - Payment amounts
  - Payment methods
  - Reference numbers

**Downloading Invoice PDF**

1. View invoice details
2. Click "Download PDF" button
3. PDF is generated and downloaded
4. PDF includes:
   - School branding
   - Invoice number and details
   - Your information (student/parent)
   - Items breakdown
   - Payment information
   - Total amounts
   - Due date

#### Understanding Invoice Status

**Status Colors and Meanings**

- **Paid** (Green): Invoice is fully paid
  - No action needed
  - Payment complete
- **Pending** (Yellow): Invoice is awaiting payment
  - Payment not yet received
  - Pay before due date
- **Unpaid** (Red): Invoice is not paid
  - Payment required
  - May or may not be past due
- **Overdue** (Red): Invoice is past due date
  - Payment is late
  - Contact finance office immediately
  - May incur penalties
- **Partially Paid** (Yellow): Some payment received
  - Partial payment made
  - Remaining balance still due
  - Complete payment to clear invoice

**Invoice Types**

- **Enrollment Invoice**: Payment for class enrollment
- **Installment Invoice**: Monthly payment for installment plan
- **Downpayment Invoice**: Initial downpayment for Installment package
- **Reservation Invoice**: Reservation fee
- **Other Invoices**: Miscellaneous charges

#### Important Notes

- Invoices are created by Admin/Superadmin
- Students cannot create or modify invoices
- Invoice status updates when payments are recorded by Finance
- Download PDFs for your records
- Red status (Unpaid/Overdue) requires attention

## Understanding Your Information

### Invoice Information

**What is an Invoice?**
- A bill for payment
- Shows what you need to pay
- Includes items, amounts, and due date

**Invoice Components**
- **Invoice Number**: Unique identifier (INV-XXXX)
- **Description**: What the invoice is for
- **Items**: Breakdown of charges
- **Amounts**: Item amounts, taxes, discounts
- **Total**: Final amount to pay
- **Dates**: Issue date and due date
- **Status**: Payment status

**Invoice Items**
- Description of charges
- Individual amounts
- Taxes (if applicable)
- Discounts (if applied)
- Penalties (if late)
- Total calculation

### Payment Status

**Understanding Status**
- **Paid**: Payment complete, no action needed
- **Pending**: Awaiting payment, pay before due date
- **Unpaid**: Payment required, check due date
- **Overdue**: Payment is late, contact finance immediately
- **Partially Paid**: Some payment made, remaining balance due

**What to Do Based on Status**
- **Paid**: Keep receipt, no action needed
- **Pending/Unpaid**: Make payment before due date
- **Overdue**: Contact finance office immediately
- **Partially Paid**: Complete remaining payment

### Class Enrollment

**Enrollment Types**
- **Full Payment**: Pay entire amount upfront
- **Installment**: Pay in monthly installments
  - Requires downpayment
  - Monthly payments
  - Progress through phases as you pay

**Enrollment Status**
- **Enrolled**: Fully enrolled, can attend classes
- **Reserved**: Spot reserved, upgrade to enrollment needed
- **Pending**: Downpayment paid, waiting for first installment

## Payment Process

### When You Receive an Invoice

**Step 1: View Invoice**
1. Check your Dashboard for pending payments
2. Go to Invoice page
3. Find the invoice
4. Click to view details

**Step 2: Review Invoice**
1. Check invoice number
2. Verify amount
3. Note due date
4. Review items
5. Download PDF for records

**Step 3: Make Payment**
1. Contact finance office
2. Provide invoice number
3. Make payment using accepted method:
   - Cash (at school office)
   - Bank Transfer
   - GCash/PayMaya
   - Check
   - Other (check with school)
4. Get receipt
5. Keep receipt for records

**Step 4: Verify Payment**
1. Wait for Finance to record payment
2. Check Invoice page - status should update to "Paid"
3. Check Payment Logs - payment should appear
4. Verify amount and reference number
5. Contact Finance if payment doesn't appear

### Payment Methods

**Accepted Methods** (check with your school):
- **Cash**: Pay at school office
- **Bank Transfer**: Transfer to school bank account
  - Get bank details from finance office
  - Include invoice number in transfer
  - Keep transfer receipt
- **GCash/PayMaya**: Mobile payment
  - Get payment details from finance office
  - Include invoice number
  - Keep transaction receipt
- **Check**: Pay by check
  - Make payable to school
  - Include invoice number
  - Keep check copy

### After Payment

**What Happens:**
1. Finance records your payment in system
2. Invoice status updates to "Paid"
3. Payment appears in Payment Logs
4. You receive payment confirmation (if email configured)
5. For enrollment invoices: You're enrolled in class
6. For installment invoices: Phase progress updates

**What You Should Do:**
1. Verify invoice status updated
2. Check Payment Logs for payment record
3. Keep your receipt
4. Download invoice PDF for records
5. Contact Finance if status doesn't update

## Enrollment Process

### Reservation Process

1. **Admin Reserves Your Spot**
   - Admin reserves your spot in a class
   - Reservation invoice is created
   - You receive notification

2. **Pay Reservation Fee**
   - View reservation invoice
   - Pay reservation fee before due date
   - Reservation is confirmed

3. **Upgrade to Enrollment**
   - Admin upgrades reservation to enrollment
   - Enrollment invoice is created
   - Pay enrollment fee

### Full Payment Enrollment

1. **Enrollment Invoice Created**
   - Admin creates enrollment invoice
   - Invoice includes package price
   - Due date is set

2. **Pay Enrollment Fee**
   - View invoice
   - Pay full amount
   - Payment is recorded

3. **You're Enrolled**
   - Upon payment, you're enrolled in class
   - You can access class schedule
   - You can attend classes
   - Attendance is tracked

### Installment Plan Enrollment

1. **Downpayment Invoice**
   - Admin enrolls you with Installment package
   - Downpayment invoice is created
   - Pay downpayment amount

2. **Pay Downpayment**
   - View downpayment invoice
   - Pay downpayment
   - Status updates to "Paid"

3. **Monthly Installments**
   - System generates monthly invoices
   - Receive invoice each month
   - Pay monthly installment
   - Progress through phases as you pay

4. **Phase Progression**
   - Pay first installment: Enrolled in Phase 1
   - Pay subsequent installments: Progress through phases
   - Complete all payments: All phases completed

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem**: Cannot see my classes
- **Solution**: 
  - Verify you're enrolled (contact Admin)
  - Check if enrollment invoice is paid
  - Refresh the page
  - Contact Admin if classes are missing

**Problem**: Invoice status not updating after payment
- **Solution**:
  - Wait a few minutes (Finance needs to record payment)
  - Refresh the page
  - Check Payment Logs to see if payment is recorded
  - Contact Finance office to verify

**Problem**: Cannot download invoice PDF
- **Solution**:
  - Check internet connection
  - Try different browser
  - Clear browser cache
  - Contact IT support

**Problem**: Payment not showing in Payment Logs
- **Solution**:
  - Verify payment was made
  - Check with Finance that payment was recorded
  - Provide receipt/reference number to Finance
  - Wait a few hours and check again

**Problem**: Invoice shows as Overdue
- **Solution**:
  - Contact Finance office immediately
  - Make payment as soon as possible
  - Check for any penalties
  - Update payment information

**Problem**: Cannot see invoice details
- **Solution**:
  - Click on invoice number to view details
  - Refresh the page
  - Check internet connection
  - Contact Admin if invoice is missing

**Problem**: Class schedule not showing
- **Solution**:
  - Check Calendar page
  - Verify you're enrolled in classes
  - Refresh the page
  - Contact Admin if schedule is wrong

**Problem**: Attendance not showing
- **Solution**:
  - Attendance is recorded by teachers
  - Check Classes page for attendance
  - Contact Teacher if attendance is missing
  - Attendance may take time to appear

### Getting Help

**For Payment Issues**:
